Overnight Developments

What changed since yesterday's close — the AI-valuation scare went global via the memory complex.

1. A global memory-chip rout — KOSPI -10%, SK Hynix & Samsung -12%, circuit breakers twice The overnight epicenter was Asia. Chip giants SK Hynix and Samsung Electronics each fell more than 12%, and Japan's Kioxia tumbled over 15%. The trigger was a Broadcom-linked AI selloff compounded by regulatory signals that the chip rally had become overheated — overseas investors dumped the names that had led the global AI rally to a record just a day earlier. 2. 3. Micron -10% premarket — its 6/24 print becomes the memory-complex decider Micron (MU) dropped ~10% in premarket trading after surging to a record close Monday on the AI-memory boom and an Anthropic deal. The reversal turns its earnings, due Wednesday 6/24 AMC, into the single most important read of the week for whether memory demand remains strong — landing now into a maximally AI-skeptic tape. A soft HBM/DRAM demand or pricing guide is the direct risk to the memory cohort (DRAM, and SKM via SK Hynix); an in-line or constructive guide could mark a capitulation low for the whole AI-capex complex.

SpaceX (SPCX) extends its rout -16% toward sub-$2T SpaceX shares fell as much as 16% premarket to ~$147 — below their first-day opening price of $150 — extending a third straight losing session and putting the company on track to slip below $2 trillion in market value. The unwind is a marquee symptom of the broader AI/space-momentum repricing.

Glossary

GEX (Gamma Exposure)
Net dealer gamma position. Positive = dealers stabilize (sell rallies, buy dips). Negative = amplify. Read qualitatively here via VIX term structure.
VIX9D
9-day implied vol on SPX. Captures the event window tighter than spot VIX (30-day). Compression vs spot = market pricing a binary.
Vanna
Sensitivity of option delta to IV. IV crush on good news forces mechanical dealer buying — "vol crush → mechanical bid."
Charm
Delta decay over time. Runs 24/7. Bleeds a call-heavy book even in a flat tape.
Magnet / Accelerator / Trapdoor
Strike vocabulary. Magnet = positive-gamma strike attracting price. Accelerator = negative-gamma strike amplifying. Trapdoor = cluster that cascades.
Contango / Backwardation
VIX curve shape. Contango (front < back) = normal. Backwardation (front > back) = stress.
MOVE
Bond-market VIX. Rising MOVE = rates vol expanding, cross-asset hedge pressure.
OVX
Oil-market VIX. Leading indicator for geopolitical repricing.
Binary
A known-date catalyst with two discrete outcomes. Requires different positioning than a trend.
